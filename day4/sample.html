Sita Navami

Sita Navami celebrates the anniversary date of the appearance or manifestation of Sita. On the occasion of Sita Navami, married women fast for their husbands's long life.
According to the Hindu scriptures, once the King Janak, who was the king of Mithila, was ploughing the field, got a pitcher and when he opened that, he found a baby girl in the pitcher.
This year, Sita Navami will be celebrated on April 29. Sita Navami is celebrated on Navami Tithi during Shukla Paksha of Vaishakha month. According to Drik Panchang, the Navami Tithi8 for Sita Navami will begin at 04:01 PM on April 28 and end at 06:22 PM on April 29.
